Rock Festival 2023

June 15, 2023 | 18:00
Central Park, New York
From $50

About This Event

Join us for the biggest rock festival of the year! Rock Festival 2023 brings together the best rock bands from around the world for an unforgettable experience.

Featuring live performances from legendary bands and emerging artists, this festival offers a diverse lineup that will satisfy all rock music lovers. From classic rock to alternative and metal, there's something for everyone.

The festival will take place at the beautiful Central Park, with multiple stages, food vendors, and merchandise stands. Don't miss this opportunity to be part of music history!

Lineup

  • 18:00 - 19:00 The Rockers Main Stage
  • 19:15 - 20:15 Electric Pulse Main Stage
  • 20:30 - 21:30 Metal Mayhem Main Stage
  • 21:45 - 23:00 Rock Legends Main Stage

Additional Information

  • Gates open at 16:00
  • All ages event (under 16 must be accompanied by an adult)
  • Food and beverages available for purchase
  • No outside food or drinks allowed
  • Limited parking available
